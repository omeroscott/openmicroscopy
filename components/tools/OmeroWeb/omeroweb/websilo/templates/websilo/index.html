OMERO/HIC is a joint project that aims to develop extensions to the OMERO environment to support data-intensive research and secure data management for healthcare informatics. This is a joint project between the Wellcome Trust Centre for Gene Regulation & Expression (GRE) and the Health Informatics Centre (HIC) at the University of Dundee.

The aim is for OMERO.server to manage healthcare datasets in a secure, audited environment ensuring that such are datasets are only used in accordance with the agreed operating procedures.

This OMERO.web application is a prototype of a web environment that will enable dataset managers to import healthcare datasets and audit subsequent usage, and for healthcare researchers to make use of the date.
